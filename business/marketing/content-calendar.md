## Posting Cadence & Engagement Tips

**Cadence**
- Post **3x per week** to start (consistency beats volume). Mon/Wed/Fri mid-morning ET works well for B2B.
- Keep a 2–3 post buffer drafted ahead so a busy week never breaks the streak.
- Reshare your strongest post each month with a fresh hook — good ideas deserve a second run.

**Format**
- Lead with a **hook in the first line** — it's the only part most people see before "…see more."
- Short paragraphs. White space. One idea per line where it helps scanning.
- End every post with **one clear CTA** — book, read, or comment. Never two.
- Use **3–5 hashtags**, a mix of broad (#TechnologyConsulting) and niche (#DevSecOps, #MidMarket).
- Native text and simple carousels outperform posts that push people off-platform — when linking out, put the link in the **first comment** if reach dips.

**Engagement**
- Reply to **every comment** within the first 1–2 hours — early engagement drives the algorithm.
- Spend 10–15 minutes commenting thoughtfully on others' posts before and after you publish.
- Have the founder and team **reshare to personal profiles** with a one-line personal take — personal reach beats page reach early on.
- Tag relevant people/partners only when it's genuinely relevant. Don't spam.
- Track what works: note which hooks, themes, and CTAs drive profile visits and booking clicks, then double down monthly.

**Measure**
- Watch profile visits, page follows, and consultation bookings — not just likes.
- Review top and bottom performers at month-end and adjust the next month's themes accordingly.
