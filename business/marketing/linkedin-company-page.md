## About Section

**From idea to operations — one partner for the whole technology lifecycle.**

B&B Global Services LLC is a technology consulting and managed services firm for mid-market and growing businesses. We help companies that have outgrown basic IT support but aren't ready for a massive consulting firm — bringing enterprise-level practices without enterprise-level complexity or cost.

Most providers stop at advice or a single project. We go further. We advise, build, secure, deploy, operate, and recover — so the same partner that designs your roadmap is the one that ships it and keeps it running. We call it our **Idea to Operations Framework**.

**What we do — six integrated service areas:**

- **Strategy & Advisory** — technology assessments, roadmaps, and fractional CTO leadership.
- **Application & Product Development** — web, mobile, SaaS, and AI-enabled applications, from MVP to platform.
- **Cloud, DevOps & Platform Engineering** — cloud architecture, Infrastructure as Code, and CI/CD on AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **DevSecOps & Cybersecurity** — security embedded across the delivery lifecycle, plus compliance readiness.
- **Reliability, Operations & BCDR** — observability, SRE practices, and tested disaster recovery.
- **IMAC & Managed Technology Services** — device deployment, service desk, network support, and managed operations.

**Who we help:**

Mid-sized companies (25–5,000 employees), startups building their first serious platform, organizations modernizing legacy systems, and private equity portfolio companies — across healthcare, financial services, SaaS and technology, professional services, real estate, and hospitality.

Most engagements begin with a focused **Technology Health Check**: a current-state review, risk analysis, and a prioritized 90-day roadmap.
