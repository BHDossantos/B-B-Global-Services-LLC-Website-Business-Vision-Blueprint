# Master Services Agreement (MSA)

> **Disclaimer:** This is a starting template, not legal advice. Have a licensed attorney review and adapt it before use.

**B&B Global Services LLC** — *From Idea to Operations*

---

## 1. Parties & Effective Date

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of **[EFFECTIVE_DATE]** (the "Effective Date") by and between:

- **B&B Global Services LLC**, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of New Hampshire, with offices in New Hampshire and Boston, MA ("Provider," "B&B," "we," "us"); and
- **[CLIENT_LEGAL_NAME]**, a [CLIENT_ENTITY_TYPE] organized under the laws of [CLIENT_STATE], with its principal place of business at [CLIENT_ADDRESS] ("Client," "you").

Provider and Client are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

---

## 2. Definitions

- **"Services"** means the technology consulting and managed services performed by B&B under one or more SOWs, including strategy and advisory, application and product development, cloud and DevOps, DevSecOps and cybersecurity, reliability and BCDR, and IMAC and managed technology services.
- **"Statement of Work"** or **"SOW"** means a document executed by both Parties describing specific Services, deliverables, fees, and timelines, governed by this Agreement.
- **"Deliverables"** means the work product B&B agrees to deliver to Client under a SOW.
- **"Confidential Information"** has the meaning set forth in Section 7.
- **"Client Data"** means data and content provided by Client or generated on Client's behalf in the course of the Services.
- **"Background IP"** means intellectual property owned or licensed by a Party prior to, or developed independently of, this Agreement.
- **"Fees"** means the amounts payable by Client as stated in a SOW.

---

## 3. Scope & Relationship to SOWs

3.1 This Agreement sets the general terms governing all Services. The specific Services are described in one or more SOWs.

3.2 Each SOW is incorporated into and governed by this Agreement. If a SOW conflicts with this Agreement, this Agreement controls, except where the SOW expressly references the conflicting section and states that it overrides.

3.3 This Agreement does not obligate either Party to enter into any SOW or to purchase or provide any minimum volume of Services.

3.4 B&B may use qualified subcontractors to perform Services and remains responsible for their performance and compliance with this Agreement.

---

## 4. Term & Termination

4.1 **Term.** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated as provided below. It remains in effect while any SOW is active.

4.2 **Termination for convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on **[30]** days' prior written notice, provided no SOW is then in effect. An active SOW may be terminated for convenience on **[30]** days' prior written notice unless the SOW states otherwise.

4.3 **Termination for cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW if the other Party materially breaches and fails to cure the breach within **[15]** days after written notice.

4.4 **Effect of termination.** Upon termination, Client will pay for all Services performed and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, including work in progress. B&B will deliver completed and in-progress Deliverables for which payment has been made and reasonably cooperate in an orderly transition.

4.5 **Survival.** Sections covering Fees, Intellectual Property, Confidentiality, Data Protection, Warranties, Limitation of Liability, Indemnification, and General Provisions survive termination.

---

## 5. Fees & Payment Terms

5.1 **Fees.** Client will pay the Fees stated in each SOW. Fees may be fixed, time-and-materials, or recurring retainer as specified.

5.2 **Invoicing.** B&B will invoice as set out in the applicable SOW. Unless a SOW states otherwise, payment is due **Net 15** from the invoice date.

5.3 **Expenses.** Client will reimburse pre-approved, reasonable out-of-pocket expenses (e.g., travel) at cost. Third-party software, licensing, and subscriptions are Client's responsibility unless stated in a SOW.

5.4 **Late payments.** Undisputed amounts not paid when due accrue interest at the lesser of **1.5%** per month or the maximum rate permitted by law. B&B may suspend Services on **[10]** days' written notice for undisputed past-due amounts.

5.5 **Taxes.** Fees are exclusive of taxes. Client is responsible for applicable sales, use, and similar taxes, excluding taxes on B&B's net income.

5.6 **Disputed amounts.** Client will notify B&B of any good-faith dispute within **[10]** days of the invoice date and pay all undisputed amounts on time. The Parties will work in good faith to resolve disputed amounts promptly.

---

## 6. Intellectual Property Ownership

6.1 **Background IP.** Each Party retains all rights to its Background IP. Nothing in this Agreement transfers Background IP except as expressly stated.

6.2 **Deliverables.** Upon full payment for the applicable Services, B&B assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the custom Deliverables created specifically for Client under the relevant SOW, excluding B&B Materials defined below.

6.3 **B&B Materials.** B&B retains ownership of its pre-existing and independently developed tools, frameworks, methodologies, templates, libraries, and know-how ("B&B Materials"). To the extent B&B Materials are embedded in a Deliverable, B&B grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use them as part of the Deliverable for Client's internal business purposes.

6.4 **Third-party and open-source components.** Deliverables may incorporate third-party or open-source components licensed under their own terms. B&B will identify material components on request.

6.5 **Client Data.** Client retains all rights to Client Data. Client grants B&B a limited license to use Client Data solely to perform the Services.

6.6 **Feedback.** B&B may use general knowledge, skills, and experience gained during the engagement, and may use anonymized, aggregated learnings, provided no Confidential Information or Client-identifying data is disclosed.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

7.1 **Definition.** "Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by one Party ("Discloser") to the other ("Recipient") that is marked confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential, including business, technical, financial, and customer information.

7.2 **Obligations.** Recipient will (a) use Confidential Information only to perform under this Agreement, (b) protect it with at least the same care it uses for its own confidential information and no less than reasonable care, and (c) limit access to personnel and subcontractors with a need to know who are bound by similar obligations.

7.3 **Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that is or becomes public without breach, was rightfully known before disclosure, is independently developed, or is rightfully received from a third party without restriction.

7.4 **Compelled disclosure.** Recipient may disclose Confidential Information if legally compelled, provided it gives reasonable prior notice (where lawful) and cooperates in seeking protective treatment.

7.5 **Term.** Confidentiality obligations continue for **[3]** years after termination, except trade secrets, which remain protected for as long as they qualify as trade secrets under applicable law.

7.6 **Return or destruction.** On request after termination, Recipient will return or destroy Confidential Information, subject to routine backup retention and legal requirements.

---

## 8. Data Protection & Security

8.1 **Safeguards.** B&B will maintain commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the Services, consistent with its DevSecOps and security practices, designed to protect Client Data against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

8.2 **Use of Client Data.** B&B will process Client Data only as necessary to perform the Services and as instructed by Client.

8.3 **Personnel.** B&B will ensure personnel and subcontractors with access to Client Data are bound by confidentiality and security obligations consistent with this Agreement.

8.4 **Incident notification.** B&B will notify Client without undue delay after confirming a security incident affecting Client Data and will reasonably cooperate in investigation and remediation.

8.5 **Compliance.** Where a SOW involves regulated data (e.g., healthcare or financial data), the Parties will agree on any additional terms, such as a data processing addendum or business associate agreement, in writing.

8.6 **Return of data.** On termination, B&B will, at Client's request, return or securely destroy Client Data in its possession, subject to routine backups and legal retention requirements.

---

## 9. Warranties

9.1 **Mutual.** Each Party warrants it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and that performance will not violate any other agreement binding on it.

9.2 **Services warranty.** B&B warrants that the Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards. Client's exclusive remedy for breach of this warranty is re-performance of the deficient Services, provided Client notifies B&B in writing within **[30]** days of the affected Services.

9.3 **Disclaimer.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, THE SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS," AND B&B DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT. B&B DOES NOT WARRANT THAT THE SERVICES OR DELIVERABLES WILL BE UNINTERRUPTED OR ERROR-FREE.

---

## 10. Limitation of Liability

10.1 **Exclusion of indirect damages.** Neither Party will be liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, or for lost profits, revenue, or data, even if advised of the possibility.

10.2 **Cap.** Each Party's total aggregate liability arising out of or related to this Agreement will not exceed the total Fees paid or payable by Client under the SOW giving rise to the claim during the **[12]** months preceding the event giving rise to liability.

10.3 **Exceptions.** The exclusions and cap do not apply to (a) Client's payment obligations, (b) either Party's indemnification obligations, (c) breach of confidentiality, or (d) a Party's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or fraud.

10.4 The limitations in this Section reflect the allocation of risk between the Parties and survive any failure of essential purpose of a limited remedy.

---

## 11. Indemnification

11.1 **By B&B.** B&B will defend and indemnify Client against third-party claims alleging that a Deliverable, as delivered by B&B, infringes a U.S. intellectual property right, and will pay damages finally awarded or settled. B&B has no obligation for claims arising from Client modifications, combination with non-B&B items, or use contrary to documentation.

11.2 **By Client.** Client will defend and indemnify B&B against third-party claims arising from Client Data, Client's materials, or Client's use of the Deliverables in violation of law or this Agreement.

11.3 **Procedure.** The indemnified Party will (a) promptly notify the indemnifying Party, (b) give it sole control of the defense and settlement (no settlement imposing obligations on the indemnified Party without consent), and (c) reasonably cooperate.

---

## 12. Insurance

During the term, B&B will maintain, at its own expense, commercially reasonable insurance for a firm of its size, which may include commercial general liability, professional liability (errors and omissions), and cyber liability coverage. B&B will provide a certificate of insurance on reasonable request.

---

## 13. Independent Contractor Status

The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, agency, or employment relationship. Neither Party may bind the other. B&B is solely responsible for its personnel, including wages, taxes, and benefits.

---

## 14. Non-Solicitation

During the term and for **[12]** months after, neither Party will knowingly solicit for employment any employee or contractor of the other who was directly involved in the Services, without the other Party's prior written consent. This does not restrict general advertising or hiring of individuals who respond to it independently.

---

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party is liable for delay or failure to perform (other than payment obligations) due to causes beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, natural disasters, war, terrorism, civil unrest, labor disputes, governmental action, utility or internet failures, and pandemics. The affected Party will notify the other promptly and use reasonable efforts to resume performance. If a force majeure event continues beyond **[30]** days, either Party may terminate the affected SOW on written notice.

---

## 16. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by and construed under the laws of the **State of New Hampshire**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles. The Parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction and venue of the state and federal courts located in New Hampshire, subject to Section 17.

---

## 17. Dispute Resolution

17.1 **Escalation.** The Parties will first attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith discussions between senior representatives within **[15]** business days of written notice of the dispute.

17.2 **Mediation.** If unresolved, the Parties will attempt non-binding mediation in [LOCATION, e.g., New Hampshire] before pursuing litigation, with costs shared equally.

17.3 **Litigation.** If mediation fails, either Party may pursue available remedies in the courts identified in Section 16. Nothing prevents a Party from seeking injunctive relief to protect Confidential Information or intellectual property.

17.4 **Fees.** [Each Party bears its own costs and attorneys' fees / The prevailing Party is entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.]

---

## 18. General Provisions

18.1 **Entire agreement.** This Agreement, together with its SOWs and any executed Change Orders, is the entire agreement and supersedes all prior understandings on the subject matter.

18.2 **Order of precedence.** In a conflict: (1) this Agreement, (2) the applicable SOW, (3) Change Orders, (4) other attachments, unless a document expressly overrides a specific section.

18.3 **Amendments.** Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

18.4 **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's prior written consent, except to a successor in a merger or sale of substantially all assets, on written notice.

18.5 **Notices.** Notices must be in writing and sent to the addresses on the signature page, by personal delivery, recognized courier, or email with confirmation.

18.6 **Waiver.** No waiver is effective unless in writing. A waiver of one breach is not a waiver of any other.

18.7 **Severability.** If any provision is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in effect and the provision is modified to the minimum extent necessary to be enforceable.

18.8 **Publicity.** Neither Party will use the other's name or marks without prior written consent, except that B&B may identify Client as a customer in general portfolio listings unless Client opts out in writing.

18.9 **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including electronic signatures, each of which is an original and together one instrument.
