Guiding principle: **lead narrow, expand broad.** The three core offers and the lead-pillar engagements (Cloud/DevOps and Reliability/BCDR) carry the go-to-market. The remaining pillars are priced and ready, fulfilled in-house or through the partner bench as demand pulls us in.

---

## 1. Packaging Principles

How we decide *how* to price, not just how much:

- **Assessments are fixed-scope, fixed-fee.** A clear deliverable (current-state review, roadmap, risk register) at a predictable price. This is what makes the Health Check a low-risk *yes*.
- **Projects are value-based, scoped to outcome.** Build and modernization work is priced to the business outcome and risk removed — not to hours. We anchor to the value of a faster release, an avoided outage, or a shipped product.
- **Operations are recurring.** Support, reliability, BCDR, and fractional leadership are retainers billed monthly. Recurring revenue is the stability metric that de-risks the founder-led model.
- **Day-rate is the fallback, not the headline.** Used for ambiguous, exploratory, or change-order work where scope can't yet be fixed. We lead with packaged outcomes and reserve T&M for the edges.
- **Always propose the operate/recover phase.** Every project should carry a path into a retainer — "no hand-off into a vacuum."

## 5. Day-Rate & Blended-Rate Guidance

For exploratory, ambiguous, or change-order work that can't be fixed-scoped, use time-based pricing. These rates back into the packaged ranges above and exist to keep T&M consistent with our value-based pricing — not to undercut it.

| Engagement mode | Indicative blended day rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Senior advisory / fractional leadership | $2,500–$3,500/day | Strategy, architecture, fractional CTO time |
| Senior engineering (cloud / DevOps / SRE) | $2,000–$3,000/day | Lead-pillar delivery; the core of most project work |
| Specialist / partner-augmented work | $1,500–$2,500/day | Capacity from the partner bench, marked up to cover oversight |
| Managed / support operations | Priced per user or per retainer | See IMAC rate card; not sold by the day |

**How to use it:** lead with a packaged, fixed-scope or value-based price wherever possible. Quote day-rate only for discovery spikes, scoping unknowns, or change orders. A fractional CTO retainer of $5,000–$25,000/month should reconcile to roughly 2–8 senior advisory days per month at the rates above.

## 6. Retainer Structures

Recurring engagements are the firm's stability layer. Bill monthly in advance. Reconcile usage quarterly and adjust the tier as the relationship grows.

| Retainer | Structure | Investment | What's covered |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Managed Technology Support** | Tiered by users / scope | $1,500–$10,000/month | User & device support, IMAC, vendor coordination, monthly reporting, basic security & backup oversight |
| **BCDR Retainer** | Fixed monthly + scheduled tests | From ~$2,500/month | Recovery-plan ownership, backup validation, tabletop exercises, recovery-test cadence, executive resilience reporting |
| **Application Maintenance** | % of build or fixed monthly | ~15–20% of build value / yr, or fixed tier | Bug fixes, dependency and security updates, minor enhancements, uptime monitoring, support handoff ownership |
| **SRE / Reliability Ownership** | Fixed monthly | Scoped from SRE engagement | Monitoring & alerting ownership, SLO/SLI upkeep, incident response, continuous reliability improvement |
| **Fractional CTO** | Fixed monthly | $5,000–$25,000/month | Technology leadership, roadmap stewardship, vendor and team oversight, board-level reporting |

**Retainer principles:**

- Every project should propose a follow-on retainer as part of the close — the highest-value motion is **project → recurring**.
- Right-size the entry tier so it's easy to say yes to, then grow it as scope and trust expand.
- Annual commitments may carry a modest discount versus month-to-month; structure to reward commitment without eroding margin.

## 7. Terms & Pass-Throughs

- **Payment terms (projects):** typically 40% on signature, 40% at midpoint milestone, 20% on acceptance. Net 15.
- **Payment terms (retainers):** billed monthly in advance. Net 15.
- **Pass-throughs:** travel, third-party software, cloud, and licensing costs are passed through at cost unless stated otherwise in the SOW.
- **Change control:** scope changes are priced via the day-rate guidance and handled through the SOW change-control process.
- **Validity:** quoted ranges are indicative; final pricing is confirmed in the Statement of Work.
